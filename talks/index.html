    The Rhythm of Smell: How Natural Breathing Structures Human Olfactory Processing

    April 11, 2026

    Talk, Stanford-Berkeley Women in CS/EE Research Meetup, Stanford University, CA, USA

    Stanford-Berkeely Research Meetup In my talk at the Stanford-Berkeley Women in EECS Research Meetup, I discussed “The Rhythm of Smell: How Natural Breathing Structures Human Olfactory Processing,” highlighting how our natural breathing patterns influence our sense of smell and overall sensory experience. This talk was aimed for a broad audience of women in CS + EE at Berkeley and Stanford.
