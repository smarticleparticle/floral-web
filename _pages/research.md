My current research investigates the neural mechanisms underlying olfactory perception and their connection to whole-body physiology. 

I'm generally interested in:

- designing affordable technologies that expand access to neuroscience tools and clinical diagnostics
- developing health monitoring devices for underserved communities
- fabricating flexible electronics for wearable physiological sensing

To do this, I combine signal processing, physiological recordings, low-cost hardware development, and flexible electronics fabrication.
