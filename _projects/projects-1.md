My sister, [Erika Hathaway](https://public.websites.umich.edu/~hathawae/), and I designed and deployed a high-altitude celestial navigation system as a backup positioning method. The platform integrated a Raspberry Pi, camera, IMU sensors, and custom signal processing algorithms to determine location from star-field imagery.
